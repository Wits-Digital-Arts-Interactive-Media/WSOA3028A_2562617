Responsive Design

by Zandile Gebuza on 06 May 2024

According to WebFX, these are the benefits of responsive design:

  • Cost effectiveness
  • Flexibility
  • Improved user experience
  • Search engine optimization gains
  • Ease of management

These are the challenges of responsive design:

  • Not every site looks great on every device
  • You could run into problems with resized advertisements or banner ads.
  • It may be difficult for you to create a responsive website on your own that is unique and accurate to your branding.
  • You may want to offer different things to different users according to which device they use, and this can be somewhat difficult on responsive websites.

I believe that because not everyone has a desktop so it's important to design websites so that they can be viewed from mobile devices. Accessibility is not about building for people living with disabilities but it is about making sure that you reach the widest audience possible.
