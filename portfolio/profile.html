Profile

Hi there! My name is Zandile Gebuza and I am 20 years old. I am a 3rd year Digital Arts student at Wits University majoring in Animation and Interactive Media. I am also a member of the Wits Golden Key Society. I speak three languages: English, IsiZulu and Sesotho but I am also interested in learning Spanish. I love the arts. I love drawing, painting and listening to music. In high school I took Visual Arts as one of my subjects and I also got the opportunity to be in a marimba band. I'm not that good at coding but last year I was able to create 8, pretty decent, games for my assignments, three of which were group work.
