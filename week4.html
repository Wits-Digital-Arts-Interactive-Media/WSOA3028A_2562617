Week 4

Notes on the IxD Process:

Goal Alignment

My goal is that I want my website to be innovative, functional, and aesthetically pleasing. Even though it might have familiar features,
I want people to find my website unique. I want them to find it easy to use and for every aspect to work the way I planned it to. And the most important
thing is, I want them to see it as beautiful and organised in a creative way.

User Flow


Information Structure

Interface Elements
  • Input controls: I will add a text field with a submit button for users to leave me a message or contact me. This will added at the bottom of my Profile page.

  • Informational Components: I will add tooltips for the images that I use to describe the contents of the image. I might also add a progress bar for people who
    view my website to see when they have viewed all the pages of my website.

  • Navigational Components: I will add navigation tabs on the top of each page. I will also add pagination on each page and at the bottom of my Profile and
    Portfolio page, I will add icons for my social media pages.

Reflection on how my original wireframes hold up in line with this process.

Now that I have learnt about the IxD Process, I have to redo my wireframes. This is because my original wireframes were very basic and I could have included more
labels and descriptions. I did not make them too detailed because I did not have that many ideas and I was new to everything we were doing. So with more
experience, ideas, as well as IxD theory, I'll have to rearrange the way I structure my wireframes and prioritise the most important things.
