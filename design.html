Wireframes

Homepage

 The homepage will have the website name at the top of the page in the middle.
Underneath it will be a navigation bar that the viewer can click to go to other pages.
The rest of the page will be a large image that represents who I am or the theme
of my website.

Blog

 The blog page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. On the left side of the page there will be the title of the
blog post. The middle of the page will be the blog post that is currently opened.
Below it there will be a button to go to the next page.

Design

 The design page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. On the left side of the page there will be images related
to the whatever information is written in the middle of the page where it says "Design
Process." Below it is a button to go to the next page. On the right is a slider to move
the page up and down.

Essays

 The essay page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. On the left side of the page will be the title of the essay
as well as an extract. The large rectangle is the essay. On the right there will be a
button to go to the next page.

Profile

 The profile page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. On the left there will be a picture of myself and on the
right/middle there will be paragraphs with my information.

Portfolio

 The portfolio page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. The large rectangles represent screenshots of my artworks,
digital art, games, and animations. Below them are "read more" buttons for viewers to see
more information about the art.
