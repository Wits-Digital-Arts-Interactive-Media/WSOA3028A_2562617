Wireframes

Homepage The homepage will have the website name at the top of the page in the middle.
Underneath it will be a navigation bar that the viewer can click to go to other pages.
The rest of the page will be a large image that represents who I am or the theme
of my website.
Blog The blog page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. On the left side of the page there will be links to other
blog posts (these are represented by the word "title"). The middle of the page will be
the blog post that is currently opened. It will look like the pages of a notebook.
On the right side there will be a button to turn the page.
Design The design page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. On the left side of the page there will be images related
to the whatever information is written in the middled the page where it says "Design
Process." Below it is a button to go to the next page. On the right is a slider to move
the page up and down.
Essays The essay page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. On the left is an extract.
The large rectangle is the essay.
The circle is the "next" button.
Profile The profile page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. There will be a picture of myself and some paragraphs of my information.
Portfolio The portfolio page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation
bar at the top right corner. The large rectangles are screenshots of my art.
Below them are "read more" buttons.
