WIREFRAMES

Homepage

The homepage will have the website name at the top of the page in the middle. Underneath it will be a navigation bar that the viewer can click to go to other pages. The rest of the page will be a large image that represents who I am or the theme of my website.

Blog

The blog page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation bar at the top right corner. On the left side of the page there will be links to other blog posts. The middle of the page will be the blog post that is currently opened (It will be made to look like the pages of a book hence there are two pages and a divider in the middle). On the right, there will be buttons to go back to the previous page as well as the next page.

Design

The design page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation bar at the top right corner. On the left side of the page there will be images related to the whatever information is written in the middle of the page where it says "Design Process." Below it are buttons to go to go back to the previous page and the next page.

Essays

The essay page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation bar at the top right corner. On the left side of the page will be the title of the essay as well as a summary. The large rectangle is the essay. On the right there will be buttons to go back to the previous page and the next page.

Profile

The profile page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation bar at the top right corner. On the left there will be a picture of myself and on the right/middle there will be paragraphs with my information. Below this there will be buttons to go back and to go to the next page.

Portfolio

The portfolio page will have the website name at the top left corner and a navigation bar at the top right corner. The large rectangles represent screenshots of my artworks, digital art, games, and animations. Below them are "read more" buttons for viewers to see more information about the art as well as a button to go to the next page.
